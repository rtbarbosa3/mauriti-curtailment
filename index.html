Glossary & technical notes
PLD
Hourly settlement price (R$/MWh) set by CCEE for each submarket. Reference for spot energy settlement in Brazil.
ONS
National System Operator. Publishes constrained-off data (curtailment) for wind and solar plants.
CCEE
Electric Energy Commercialization Chamber. Publishes hourly PLD and operates the spot market.
NE
Northeast submarket of the SIN (National Interconnected System), where Mauriti is located.
REN 1.030
ANEEL Resolution 1.030/2022. Defines eligibility of curtailment for compensation. REL and CNF reasons are eligible; ENE (energy) and PAR (access opinion) generally are not.
CF (%)
Curtailment Factor. Curtailed energy divided by reference (estimated) generation, expressed as %.
REL / CNF / ENE / PAR
Curtailment reason codes. REL = external unavailability, CNF = reliability, ENE = energy (oversupply), PAR = access opinion limit.
LOC / SIS
Restriction origin codes. LOC = local (constraint near the plant — transmission line, substation or bay; a specific transmission asset is responsible). SIS = systemic (regional grid constraint — typically saturated inter-regional export limits or stability requirements; affects multiple plants simultaneously).
GHI
Global Horizontal Irradiance. Total solar power received per square meter on a horizontal surface. From NASA POWER reanalysis.
Modulation discount
Difference between actual revenue (Σ MWhh × PLDh) and "flat" revenue (MWhday × PLDdaily avg). Captures the opportunity cost of generating during low-PLD hours.

Sources Constrained-off Operating Restriction (ONS, semi-hourly base, plant detail + consolidated with reasons). Hourly PLD (CCEE). Solar irradiance (NASA POWER, hourly reanalysis at plant coordinates).

Definitions Curtailment = max(0, val_geracaoestimada − val_geracaoverificada). Lost revenue = curtailment_MWh × hourly_PLD. CF = curtailment / expected (%). Modulation discount = (Σ MWhh × PLDh) − (Σ MWhday × PLDdaily_avg).

NE benchmark Modulation benchmark aggregates all UFVs of NE submarket as a single fleet weighted by generation. Shows the discount the NE fleet collectively suffered.
